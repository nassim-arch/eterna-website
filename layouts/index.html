Our Thesis

Decentralized protocols, programmable value layers, and AI-native infrastructure are converging to reshape how we build and interact online. We invest at this intersection - backing technical founders from inception through scale, with deep conviction in the infrastructure that will power the next era of the internet.

Markets shift, narratives change, and macro conditions evolve. We help founders maintain strategic clarity through uncertainty.
