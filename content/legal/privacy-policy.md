<h2>3. Cookie Policy</h2>
<h3>What are cookies and what do they do?</h3>
<p>A cookie is a small text document, which often includes an anonymous unique identifier. When you visit a website, that website asks your computer for permission to store a file in a part of your hard drive specifically designated for cookies.</p>

<p>Information gathered through cookies and web server logs may include the date and time of visits, the browser software and operating system used to access the page, the pages viewed and the time spent on our Website.</p>

<p>For further details on cookies, please visit <a href="https://www.allaboutcookies.org/" target="_blank" rel="noopener">All About Cookies</a> where you can find more information on cookies and similar relevant technologies.</p>

<p>By continuing to use our Website, you are deemed to consent to our use of the cookies described in this Cookie Policy section.</p>
