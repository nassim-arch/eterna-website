Our Thesis

Decentralized protocols, programmable value layers, and AI-native infrastructure are converging to reshape how we build and interact online. We invest at this intersection — backing technical founders from inception through scale, with deep conviction in the infrastructure that will power the next era of the internet.

We help founders ship their products

We support founders in translating ambitious technical visions into products that users love. From testnet to mainnet, from beta to scale.

We help founders scale their business

Beyond product, we help portfolio companies build sustainable businesses — revenue models, go-to-market strategy, and operational excellence.

We help founders grow their community

In network-effect businesses, community is everything. We help founders cultivate engaged ecosystems of developers, users, and evangelists.

We help founders navigate market cycles

Markets shift, narratives change, and macro conditions evolve. We help founders maintain strategic clarity through uncertainty.
